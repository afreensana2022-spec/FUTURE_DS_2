The Core Metric: Out of 7,043 total customers, 1,869 have left. This gives an overall churn rate of 26.54%—meaning nearly 3 out of every 10 customers walk away.

The High-Risk Segment: For customers on a Month-to-Month contract, the situation is critical. They account for 1,655 out of the 1,869 total churned users (roughly 88.5% of all churn).

The Critical First 6 Months: For users with less than 6 months of tenure, the churn rate is 52.94%. Essentially, more than 5 out of 10 new sign-ups leave almost immediately. Once a customer hits the 12-month mark, that risk drops down to just 1.7 out of 10 users (17.13%).
